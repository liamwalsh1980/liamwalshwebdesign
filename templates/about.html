About Me, My Skills, the
Tech used and what I
offer as a Web Designer

I'm a fully qualified Website Designer with a level 5 Diploma in Software Development. I achieved my qualification by studying with Code Institute - a leading coding educator. During my course I was offered the position of Front-End Developer with Scolmore International , a leading manufacturer, supplier and distributor of electrical products and accessories, worldwide.

I now design, improve, and maintain the Scolmore Group websites, and with great success and many projects completed, after 6 months, I was promoted to Software Developer.

Focus

My main skills focus on Web Application Development and User Experience Design. This is where I can offer freelance services to any business or individual that requires a website to be built or at least a basic web page presence on the net.

My goal when building a website is to make sure that visitors to the site have a positive and easy-to-use experience. My dedication and work ethic will give you assurance that I can deliver the best in quality, the right style, and a cost-effective solution. I pride myself on having an eye for detail and never afraid to change something at any point to improve the overall performance of a site. My Portfolio is available to view and is regularly updated with completed projects.

Tech

I always look to use the latest and safest Software Tech. I know this will give you full peace of mind when your site is being built. I'm always looking to improve and deliver the best results, therefore, working with a great Dev team at Scolmore, I'm kept well informed of which new Tech to use whilst staying connected to the dev social media world to be aware of the latest versions of software.

Services

In order to offer the best service, I would recommend an initial consultation to make sure your site is built the way you want it. This gives you the chance to express what styling, information and images you want to display and together we can make your site stand out from the crowd.

Savings

With cost effective solutions for all budgets and fixed deadlines that works for you; I will endeavour to exceed your expectations throughout the whole process from the initial consultation through to launch, along with the aftercare which is included.
